Important notes

The world has a history with cancel culture, it became mainstream in the 20th century with incidents like McCarthyism trials and Hollywood, and the advent of social media.

People change over time. In the age of Twitter, a company would fire an employee off a bad tweet sent 10 years ago. This tool aims to dig out posts like that, but also to serve as a reminder that context matters and people change. Over the course of 10 years, someone may no longer hold this belief. You should test them on this. They might no longer hold the belief. If they still do, you can consider firing them. Context matters as well.
